Essential Aftercare

01

Clean: Spray with sterile saline solution twice daily. Do not use alcohol or peroxide.

02

Hands Off: Never touch, rotate, or twist your jewelry during the healing process.

03

Dry: Gently pat dry with a clean paper towel. Avoid cloth towels which harbor bacteria.
